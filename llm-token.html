What is a Token? 什么是Token?

A basic unit for AI models to process text, which can be a word, part of a word, or a character. For GPT models, 1 token is approximately 4 English characters or 1 Chinese character. AI模型处理文本的基本单位，可以是单词、单词的一部分或单个字符。对于GPT模型，1个Token大约等于4个英文字符或1个中文字符。
